What I offer

Two ways I can help your operation.

Whether you need guidance on what the regulations require, a digital system to keep your records audit-ready, or both — the service is tailored to your farm.

Part A — Compliance Guidance

An on-farm session to review your current practices against the Agriculture Victoria food safety requirements. Walk away with a clear action plan and no surprises at audit time.

Part B — Digital Record System

A simple, phone-friendly system that records your four key activities — Spray, Planting, Harvesting, and Delivery — and connects them into a one-click traceability report.

No software licences required. The system runs on tools you already have access to — accessible from any phone or browser, nothing to install.
